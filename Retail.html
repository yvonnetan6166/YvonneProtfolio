Retail Business Analysis

Overview

Adventure Works (AW) is a big company that makes and sells metal and composite bicycles in North America, Europe, and Australia. It's based in Bothell, Washington, and has 290 employees. They also have regional sales offices in different countries. In 2000, AW bought a plant in Mexico called Importadores Neptuno (IN) that makes parts for their bicycles. These parts are sent to Bothell, where they put everything together to make the final bicycles. In 2001, IN became the only place that makes and sells touring bicycles.

In this retail analysis, we'll use Power BI to explore sales, costs, and profits for resellers and online customers, focusing on bicycles and metals-related products sold globally. We'll use various techniques like RFM model, Pareto Analysis, Market Basket Analysis, and Rating to gain valuable insights from the data.

Business Problem/Opportunity

The primary business problem is the need to increase revenue and improve profitability in the retail business. The analysis will help identify areas for improvement and opportunities for growth.

Business Dimensions

The analysis will cover the following dimensions:

  • Sales channels: reseller and regular online customers
  • Products: bicycles and metals related
  • Sales territories: global

    • Process (Methodology)

    1. Attracting business requirements from business stakeholders and convert requirements into measurable outcome.
    2. Using Power BI Query Editor to clean and transform data, constructing a robust data model with 5-dimensional tables and 1 fact table. apply DAX and calculation groups to create essential metrics within Power BI.
    3. Visualize data in trend dashboards, incorporating slicers and KPIs to cater to different perspectives from various groups, such as VP of Sales, Finance Manager, Product Manager, Marketing Director, etc.
    4.RFM (Recency, Frequency, Monetary) model to analyze customer behavior and identify high-value customers
    5.Pareto Analysis to identify the top-selling products and customers
    6.Market Basket Analysis to identify associations between products and discovering cross-selling opportunities by understanding which items are frequently purchased together.
    7.Rating technique to analyze customer satisfaction and loyalty to gain insights into their level of contentment with the products or services offered.

    Data Relationship Diagram

    Expected Outcomes and Benefits

    The expected outcomes and benefits of this analysis include:

  • Improved understanding of sales performance across sales channels, products, and sales territories
  • Identification of high-value customers and opportunities for personalized marketing
  • Identification of top-selling products and customers, and cross-selling opportunities
  • Improved decision-making through data-driven insights
  • Increased customer satisfaction and loyalty through targeted marketing and improved customer experiences

    • Conclusion and Recommendations

    Based on the analysis, we recommend the following actions:

  • Implement personalized marketing campaigns for high-value customers identified through the RFM model
  • Focus on cross-selling opportunities for top-selling products identified through Pareto Analysis
  • Optimize product bundles and promotions based on Market Basket Analysis
  • Implement strategies to improve customer satisfaction and loyalty based on Rating technique
  • Expand sales territories in high-growth markets to drive sales and revenue

    • In conclusion, this retail analysis provides valuable insights into sales, costs, and profits across sales channels, products, and sales territories. If the retail store follows the suggestions, the retail business can improve revenue and profitability, and achieve greater success. Using Power BI, and the RFM model, Pareto Analysis, Market Basket Analysis, and Rating techniques, we were able to understand the data and help the business stay ahead of the competition.
